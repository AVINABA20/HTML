ROBO-WARS :

This is a fun game full of childhood memories similar to that of SNAKE N LADDER game.But this time itâ€™s something different there are some rule n regulations set. The participants must come in a team of 2 members.

RULES :

op 10 teams will be selected from qualifier round of written quiz. ( multiple choice questions)

Main Round:

Teams: Top 10 teams, each team must have 2 members.5 extra team next to 10 top team can be added if they want to play but they can play after 10 min
Objective: reach 100th square block in a snakes and ladder kind of layout - answering questions asked by the volunteer assigned to them. Each team will be given a set of 10 questions in each round and will be assigned a volunteer who will examine their answers and progress. The volunteer will be responsible in examining the answers of one team member as well as monitoring the steps moved by the other team player.
Round 1: 10 questions Points System: correct answer 1st attempt: 6 points (move 6 steps ahead) 2nd attempt: 4 points Correct answer with hint: 3 points (move 3 steps ahead) Negative answer: -2 points (step back 2 steps) Skip Question: 1 point deducted step back 1 steps
Round 2: 10 questions Points System: correct answer: 4 points (move 4 steps ahead) Correct answer with hint: 2 points (move 2 steps ahead) Negative answer: -1 points (step back 1 steps) Skip Question: No points and no Movement.
Round 3: 10 questions Points System: correct answer: 5 points (move 6 steps ahead) Correct answer with hint: 2 points (move 3 steps ahead) Negative answer: -1 points (step back 2 steps) Skip Question: No points and no Movement. Throughout the steps there will be special blocks which will boost them to ahead steps (LADDER) and (SNAKES) which will demote them to lower steps. All total:10 hints are free..afterward 10/- will be charged per hint.

JUDGING CRITERIA:

Teams completing the objective will be awarded 1st, 2nd and 3rd.
