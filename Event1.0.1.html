CRYPTICS :

This event analyses your analytical and aptitude skills and is a proper combination of intelligence with smartness.

RULES :

1.A team must consist 2-4 members. 2.There will be 2 rounds in the event. 3.First round will have quiz questions related to current affairs, general knowledge and general aptitude. 4.Second round will be task oriented. You will be assigned a question/task that you have to complete within the time limits.

JUDGING CRITERIA :

1.8 teams will be eligible for the second round and their rank will be decided based on the marks secured in round 1. 2.The winners will be decided on the basis of round 1 and round 2. 3.In case of a tieup there will be a provision for a third round, the rules for which will be decided by the jury. 4.In case of any discrepancy the coordinator's decision will be final.
